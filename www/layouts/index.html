Centralized Documentation

Your documentation lives across repositories, wikis, documentation sites, internal tools, and knowledge bases. Hyaline extracts and unifies information from all these sources into a single, searchable dataset that stays current as your documentation is updated.

Frequently Asked Questions

We believe documentation works best when it's accessible to everyone who needs it. By pricing per company rather than per seat, we remove barriers to collaboration and ensure that anyone in your organization can contribute to and benefit from your documentation. You shouldn't have to choose between comprehensive documentation and your budget--with Hyaline, you get both.

You can use Hyaline without a license for evaluation and demonstration purposes only. For production use, a license is required. We offer free licenses for open source projects, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions--just reach out to request one. All other organizations need to purchase a license for continued use beyond evaluation.

To make your experience with Hyaline as seamless as possible. We don't want you to have to spend time hunting for license files, dealing with activation issues, or managing renewals across your team. We trust our users to purchase appropriate licenses, and in return, you get to focus on what matters--making your documentation useful.

While Hyaline is source-available (you can view and modify the code), we've chosen a proprietary license model to ensure we can sustainably develop and support the product long-term. This approach allows us to dedicate resources to making Hyaline the best documentation tool possible while maintaining fair pricing and offering free licenses to qualifying organizations.
